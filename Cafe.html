Cafe
What’s on the menu

Confectionary

Savor delightful flavors of traditional wagashi creations! From the refreshing An-mitsu to the sweet, chewy goodness of Daifuku and Dorayaki, our collection is a true celebration of Japanese desserts.

Pan

This is Japan’s beloved take on fluffy, yeasted milk bread. Made using the tangzhong method (a water roux technique that makes the bread incredibly soft and tender), our Pan selection includes favorites like Anpan (filled with sweet red bean paste), Cream Pan, and rich, pillowy Shokupan. And if you're in the mood for something savory, don't miss our Karepan, filled with flavorful Japanese curry and fried to perfection.

Wagashi

These handcrafted Japanese sweets aren’t just delicious—they’re a piece of cultural heritage. Passed down through generations, wagashi making is a rare skill often taught only within families in Japan. That’s how deeply rooted and special this craft is. Enjoy the beauty, flavor, and tradition of these incredible treats, such as Warabi Mochi, Mochi, Daifuku, Taiaki, Dorayaki, An Mitsu, Kakigori and Misu Yokan.

Yogashi

These Western-style sweets are made with familiar ingredients like wheat flour, butter, and sugar, but infused with a uniquely Japanese twist. Think fluffy cakes and buttery pastries enhanced with flavors like matcha, Japanese cherries, and premium Japanese butter. Whether you’re craving something classic or curious to try a new fusion of flavors, our yogashi selection offers the perfect balance of comfort and surprise. Yogashi on the menu include Naruto, Fruit Cake, Kamakura, Cream Puffs, Ice Cream, Castella and Japanese Cheesecake.

Ice Cream

Enjoy different flavors of Japanese ice cream! Whether you enjoy it on the spot, in a cozy cone, or grab a cup to go, every scoop is a delicious journey into creamy, dreamy goodness.

Sweets

Dive into a selection of irresistible treats, like our rich Naruto, fluffy Shortcake, decadent Kamakura, and melt-in-your-mouth Japanese Cheesecake.

Baked

Treat yourself to the delicate sweetness of Castella, the soft, fluffy texture of Melon Pan, the rich, creamy goodness of Cream Pan, and the satisfying indulgence of An-Pan.

Nibbles

Craving something savory? Our selection of snack-sized dishes is perfect for satisfying your hunger, with no need for cutlery! Enjoy the warm, comforting flavors of a Shokupan Hot Sandwich, the delicious twist of an Okome Burger, or the simple, hearty goodness of a Shokupan Sandwich.

Healthy Treats

Our healthy options are perfect for any time of day! Enjoy the vibrant flavors of our Chia Pudding, energizing Acai Bowl, crunchy Granola Bowl, or refreshing Fruit Bowl—all made with fresh, wholesome ingredients.

Herbals

Explore a delightful variety of teas, from rich black and refreshing green to soothing herbal and aromatic blends. And, of course, don't miss out on our signature hot matcha, a traditional Japanese favorite that promises to warm both your body and soul.

Specialty Coffee

Discover a carefully curated selection of coffee, crafted with precision and passion. From rich, classic brews to unique specialty flavors, each cup is a masterpiece, prepared with skill and attention to detail.

Cold Brews

Chill out with our refreshing cold-brewed teas! We offer a variety of herbal and aromatic blends, perfect for a cool and relaxing experience. Plus, try our unique twist on the traditional hot matcha—now served cold for a fresh take on this beloved Japanese classic.

Artisanal Sodas

Experience the taste of Japan with artisanal sodas. Completely organic, with no refined sugars, these sodas are made in the UAE, but each sip offers a delightful taste of Japanese tradition, making it the perfect way to refresh and treat yourself!
